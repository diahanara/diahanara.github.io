About Me

Hello

Hi! I’m Nur Diah Rahmawati, a fresh graduate in Information Systems from Universitas Ahmad Dahlan with a passion for technology, design, and creating meaningful digital experiences. I specialize in UI/UX Design, with hands-on experience in redesigning apps, developing component foundations, and collaborating on impactful projects like BebasJualan.com and innovative applications for health and lifestyle. Beyond my academic and professional journey, I’m an active member of communities like Rumah Orkestra Jogja and Gendhing Bahana UAD , where I nurture my love for music and culture. From performing at national gamelan parades to playing intricate orchestra pieces, creativity flows in all I do. Let’s connect and craft something amazing together! 🚀
